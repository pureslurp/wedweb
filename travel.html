Local favorites

While you’re here

If you’re extending your trip or looking for something to do between events, here are a few spots we love around the area.

Distances are approximate from the Auburn Hills Marriott Pontiac.

Auburn Hills

Nearby
  • HUB Stadium Entertainment

    Axe throwing, mega darts, archery, and more—plus a full kitchen and bar. Walk-ins welcome in Auburn Hills.

  • Top Golf Entertainment

    Climate-controlled hitting bays, high-tech games, and a sports bar—fun for groups of up to six per bay.

Royal Oak

~11 mi
  • Cantaritos Restaurant & Bar

    Authentic Mexican bar & grill with traditional recipes, handcrafted cocktails, and live music on weekends.

  • HopCat Restaurant & Bar

    Craft beer, Cosmik Fries, and sidewalk patios on Main Street—plus happy hour, brunch, and trivia nights.

Birmingham

~10 mi
  • Terra Restaurant & Bar

    New American cuisine and craft cocktails in a warm, welcoming downtown Birmingham setting.

  • Dick O’Dow’s Restaurant & Bar

    A Birmingham Irish pub built on old-world hospitality—pub fare, whiskey, and good company for over 20 years.

Lake Orion

~12 mi
  • Sagebrush Cantina Restaurant & Bar

    Family-owned since 1996, known for generous Mexican plates, margaritas, and one of Michigan’s largest tequila selections.

Detroit

~28 mi
  • MGM Grand Detroit Entertainment

    Downtown resort with casino gaming, multiple restaurants, live shows at the Event Center, and a luxury hotel.

  • MotorCity Casino Entertainment

    Casino hotel with table games, dining from Revel Steak to casual bites, and live concerts at Sound Board.

  • Greektown Casino Entertainment

    Gaming, dining, hotel, and sportsbook in the heart of downtown Greektown.
